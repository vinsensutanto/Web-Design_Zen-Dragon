est. 2024

In the heart of the majestic Dragon Valley, nestled amidst towering mountains and lush forests, lies the renowned Dragon Academy. Founded centuries ago by the great dragon scholar, Professor Eldorin, the academy stands as a beacon of wisdom and discovery in the realm of Arcadia. Legend has it that Eldorin, a wise and adventurous dragon, sought to create a place where dragons and humans could come together to learn from one another. With this vision in mind, he enlisted the help of the noblest dragons and the most learned scholars to establish the academy.
